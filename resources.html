  Some take-aways of family yarns (PDF files)

  • "Our Story Thus Far. The Migrations of Our Mouser Ancestors" by Gerald F. Mouser
    About the descendants of Michel Mauser and Maria Schneider from Öschingen in Württemberg, Germany with a nice write-up about what it might have been like to live in Germany in the 17th Century and migrate to America in the 18th.
  • "Pfeffer Trials and Triumphs" by Collin, Selwyn and Allan Pfeffer
    The Story of the Pfeffers from Neckarsulm, Germany to Francis Victor and Friederike Christine (nee Schulz) at Kulgun, Queensland and their children and grandchildren with genealogical table to 1.3.1982.
  • "The Lobegeiger Family Reunion" by various authors
    Tells the story of the Lobegeigers who migrated to Queensland from Prussia in the 19th century. Starts with Wilhelm Lobegeiger and Caroline Witte and works its way down through the descendants. Brief but comprehensive.
  • "History of Thomas and Harriet Fenwick" by Neil and Linda Sivyer
    Well researched and illustrated document of our Fenwick ancestors from some of our cousins. They shared the same history but with a different story. Brings in some other interesting details from other parts of the family.
  • "Down Memory Lane" by Hazel Fenwick
    Not our family of Fenwicks but they were rubbing shoulders with ours. Has a great write-up about the history from another perspective of the Fenwicks who migrated from Scotland to Australia.
